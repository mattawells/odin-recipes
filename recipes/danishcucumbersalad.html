Danish Cucumber Salad

Description

Danish cucumber salad is a quick and really easy way to enjoy pickled cucumber. No cooking is involved and in just a few hours you will have wonderful pickled cucumber, perfect with meat dishes, especially roast

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sliced cucumber (peeled, thinly)
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ground white pepper

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, water, sugar and salt in a bowl and stir until salt and sugar is dissolved. Season with pepper to taste.
  2. Slice the cucumber very thinly. Squeeze the slices lightly between your hands to get rid of excess water. Then place them in the vinegar mixture.
  3. Leave to pickle for at least 15 minutes (if you are in a hurry). Otherwise leave for 2 hours.
  4. Serve with swedish meat balls, norwegian pancakes or your favorite viking beverage.
