Norwegian Pancakes

Description

Traditional Norwegian pancakes that any Norse god or mere mortal would love. Served like a crepe with your favorite Scandinavian berries or jam.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the eggs and milk in of a blender. Add the flour, salt and sugar, and blend until smooth.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat, and coat with cooking spray or butter.
  3. Scoop about 1/4 cup of batter into the skillet, and tilt the pan to coat the bottom with the batter.
  4. Cook until the top looks dry, about 30 seconds.
  5. Carefully slide a spatula under the pancake and flip. Cook for a few seconds on the other side, just until browned.
  6. Remove to a plate, and repeat with remaining batter.
